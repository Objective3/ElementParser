I have to say, this list is on pretty frequent rotation. It probably changes on a daily basis. But five that stayed on it longer than most are:

5. "Saturday Night's Alright (For Fighting)" by either Elton John or Nickelback, depending on what kind of party you're at

4.  "Get This Party Started" by Pink

3. "Smooth Criminal" by Michael Jackson or Alien Ant Farm or FORK (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fBnNuAFt0Ek)

2. "All The Small Things" by Blink-182

1. "Dragostea din tei" by O-zone (i,e, the Numa Numa song).

I defy anyone not to dance like a crazy person to any of those songs.

And I'd like to thank the posters of this community as a whole for generally leaving the "theme dance" songs (YMCA, the Electric Slide, the Macarena, the Chicken Dance, Souldja Boy, etc) OFF of their Top 5 Lists. this week. I now have a little more hope for humanity than I used to.
